title=Spongie's Character Customisation
description=[h2][b]Adds a new menu to character creation with more customisation options[/b][/h2]
description=This is a framework for mods that is intended to feel as vanilla as possible.
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Supported by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3340255334]Spongie's Character Retexture[/url] - Adds 12 new face options[/h1]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Supports B41 and B42. Works in multiplayer[/h1]
description=[h2][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3626418462]B41 Legacy Version here[/url][/h2]
description=
description=[b]Should be loaded after any mods that add new body locations.
description=Needs to be loaded after the Tattoo Parlour mod.
description=
description=Enabling retextures that use this mod requires a restart.
description=[/b]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Features[/h1]
description=[list]
description=    [*][b]Select different face textures[/b]
description=
description=    [*][b]Add details to your character[/b]
description=
description=    [*]Works on all skintones
description=
description=    [*][b]Visible muscles[/b] depending on strength level. Can be changed in sandbox settings
description=
description=    [*]Replaces chest hair with body hair for both body types. Now separate from the body texture
description=
description=    [*]Replaces stubble. Now has a different texture for darker skintones
description=
description=    [*][b]Stubble and body hair grow in-game and can be shaved[/b]. Can be changed in sandbox settings
description=
description=    [*][b]Mod Support[/b]
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Included[/h1]
description=[list]
description=    [*][b]Base mod[/b] - Framework, doesn't add any customisation options
description=
description=    [*][b]Vanilla Faces Pack[/b] - Adds 4 male and 4 female faces. Only supports the vanilla body textures
description=
description=    [*][b]Details Pack[/b] - Adds 37 details including: freckles, moles, scars, burns, and missing eyes.
description=	Missing eyes also affect the players vision in B42 (sandbox option)
description=
description=    [*][b]Details Pack HD Textures[/b] - 512x512 textures for the Details Pack.
description=Do not use this with the vanilla body textures or any retextures that are 256x256 or it will cause seams.[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Works in existing saves[/h1]
description=Joining a game with a character from before the mod was added will open the character customisation window.
description=
description=Stubble and chest hair will automatically be converted.
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Change Customisation In-Game[/h1]
description=There is a sandbox option to allow players to open the customisation window and change their face or details in-game.
description=
description=[b]The buttons to change your appearance and body hair are in the character info panel.[/b]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Sandbox Options[/h1]
description=Admins are always able to change their appearance, but they can spawn an [b]Appearance Changing Spiffo[/b] item which gives this ability to players when held.
description=
description=There is also a sandbox option to hide customisation options with specific categories from normal players. Admins can spawn an [b]Appearance Unlocking Bear[/b] item which will unhide these options when held.
description=
description=Body hair growth and visible muscles can be enabled or disabled - but there is also an option where players can toggle them on their character from the customisation window.
description=
description=There is also the option to set if missing eyes will affect the players vision cone. This is exclusive to B42
description=
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Mod Support[/h1]
description=I've tried my best to make character customisation as moddable as possible - new faces and details can be easily inserted into the mod.
description=
description=The body hair textures used in this mod will cut off at the underwear which will look broken when using a nude mod.
description=
description=[b]I have included nude versions of the body hair textures that you can freely use.[/b] There are 256 and 512 resolution files.
description=
description=You can add any clothing item as a face or detail option as long as they use an appropriate bodylocation that won't collide with other items.
description=
description=You can also add fullbody textures but they will appear above muscles.
description=
description=All of the functionality for getting and setting a characters customisation is accessible which should hopefully make it easy to add support for NPC mods.
description=
description=[h3]See the readme and Example.lua for an explanation of the mods features[/h3]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Compatibilities[/h1]
description=[list]
description=    [*]Makeup
description=
description=    [*]Ellie's Tattoo Parlor
description=
description=    [*]Any remodel mods
description=
description=    [*]Any retexture that includes replacement textures for this mod
description=
description=You can disable muscles in sandbox settings if the retexture you are using does not support it
description=
description=Nude mods will need to replace the body hair textures to avoid it cutting off at the underwear line
description=
description=    [*]Improved Hair Menu
description=
description=    [*]Bigger Character Avatars - Recommended
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Incompatibilities[/h1]
description=[list]
description=    [*][b]Some clothing mods might cause transparency issues with customisation. This is caused by having a broken masks folder that is missing textures for bodyparts. To fix this you can copy the missing textures into the masks folder from media\textures\Body\Masks[/b]
description=
description=    [*]Mods that affect stubble or chest hair will probably cause errors (Ras' Body Mod, Reorganised Info Screen, etc)
description=
description=    [*]Reorganised Info Screen
description=
description=    [*]Dynamic Body should probably work fine if you disable visible muscles
description=
description=    [*]Retextures that replace the chest hair textures with new skins won't be accessible because vanilla chest hair is replaced with a separate texture
description=[/list]
description=[/quote]
description=
description=[quote]
description=[h1]Known issues[/h1]
